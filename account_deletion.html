Account Deletion

Dream Journal Ultimate

Instructions on how to delete your account and information about what happens to your data.

How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your Dream Journal Ultimate account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Email team@dreamjournal.co from your registered email address.
  2. Include your account information in the email.
  3. Specify in the email that you wish to delete your account.

Data Deletion Policy

Upon the deletion of your account:

  • All data related to your account will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • This includes all entries, preferences, and other personal data associated with your account.
  • Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
